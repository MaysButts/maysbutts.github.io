    Herpes Prevention Project


Herpes Prevention Project better known as HPP is an organization that has a main goal of spreading prevention methods of Herpes to young adults. By utilizing modernized technology we believe that we will be able to get our goals accomplished and continue to keep an ongoing impact on young adults. For greater details of our organization please head over to our “Project” page for a deeper look into our individual lives you may also visit our “About Us” page.
